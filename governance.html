Governance & Ethics

Trust scales when rules are explicit. This module defines data boundaries, consent expectations, authorship norms, and a lightweight way to resolve conflicts.

Minimum rules (start here)

  • Data boundary: what can be shared, where, and under what license/NDA.
  • Consent & safety: human data, clinical workflows, and risk review triggers.
  • Authorship: contribution tracking and acknowledgement expectations.
  • Conflict resolution: escalation path + who decides + how it’s logged.
  • Long-term stewardship: maintainers, continuity, and access control.

Source doc (optional, versioned plain-text): Open governance.md

Future-proofing (next iteration)

Once collaborations increase, consider adding: decision logs, a small review group, templates for data-sharing agreements, and a public changelog of governance updates.
