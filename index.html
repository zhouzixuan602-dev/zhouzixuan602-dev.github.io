How it works

A simple loop: encode → match → collaborate → ship → feed back into shared knowledge.

1

Encode

Use structured templates to describe needs/capabilities (constraints, deliverables, proof).

2

Match

Find counterpart teams by domain: IC design, packaging, signal processing, clinical, manufacturing.

3

Collaborate

Spin up a project thread with milestones, roles, governance constraints, and escalation paths.

4

Ship & share

Publish non-sensitive learnings, specs, and postmortems—improving the network over time.
