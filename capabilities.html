Capabilities

What you (or your team) can reliably deliver—so the network can match needs to doers. Keep it concrete: scope, constraints, lead time, and typical deliverables.

What to include

  • One-line headline: “We can deliver X within Y weeks.”
  • Inputs needed: data, specs, samples, protocols, approvals.
  • Outputs: files, reports, prototypes, code, validation results.
  • Acceptance criteria: what “done” means (metrics, tests, review).
  • Availability: time window, turnaround time, response channel.

Source doc (if you still want a versioned plain-text backing): Open capabilities.md
